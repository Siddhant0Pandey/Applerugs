Our Story
Unlike many other industries, the allure of the rug industry holds a unique charm.

At AppleRugs, creating each rug involves intricate handwork, making every piece unique. This process not only showcases artistry but also provides livelihoods for many, helping families thrive. It blends creativity, cultural appreciation, and a commitment to preserving traditional hand-weaving techniques—values that inspired us to start AppleRugs. Each rug tells a story, passed down through generations, bringing together timeless craftsmanship and modern design. Our artisans pour their heart into every knot, ensuring that each rug is a masterpiece, designed to bring warmth, beauty, and tradition into your home while supporting communities and keeping a centuries-old craft alive.

How AppleRugs Started

How AppleRugs Started?

In July 2016, Baburam Dangi launched Apple Rugs with a dream to revolutionize the carpet industry. Focused on sustainability, they began weaving eco-friendly rugs using natural handspun yarn like nettle, hemp, and cactus, sourced locally from Nepal’s rural areas. Joined by his sister, Sirjana Dangi, the duo aimed to generate employment and promote traditional craftsmanship. Today, Apple Rugs is celebrated internationally for its beautiful and sustainable carpets, serving hotels, wholesalers, and interior designers while championing eco-conscious practices and community empowerment.

From rural roots to global acclaim

Giving Back

At AppleRugs, we believe that sustainability is crucial for a better future. Unfortunately, many communities still face challenges related to environmental preservation.

To address this, we have committed to using eco-friendly materials and promoting sustainable production practices.

We are also passionate about supporting local artisans. We recognize the importance of preserving traditional craftsmanship and empowering artisans to showcase their skills on a global stage. Through our initiatives, we’ve collaborated with numerous local communities, providing fair wages and opportunities for growth.

Giving back to the environment and the communities we work with is at the heart of AppleRugs.
