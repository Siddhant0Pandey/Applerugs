Persian Knot

Originating from Persia (modern-day Iran), the Persian knot is renowned for creating intricate, luxurious carpets. This technique allows for precision in weaving detailed patterns, making it ideal for artistic designs like floral motifs and medallions.

Why It’s Unique:

The Persian knot is perfect for fine, detailed patterns and is often used in carpets with sharp borders and intricate designs. The result is a smooth, dense rug that’s long-lasting and durable.

Characteristics:

  • Precision in design and pattern details.
  • Fine textures with soft, smooth surfaces.
  • Typically used for floral, medallion, and intricate border patterns.

Durability:

Persian knot rugs are known for their longevity due to the tight weave and high-quality materials used. These rugs perform well in high-traffic areas, maintaining their structure and beauty over many years.

Tibetan Knot

Tibetan rugs are handcrafted using a traditional method passed down through generations in the Himalayan region. The Tibetan knot is known for its intricate craftsmanship and luxurious texture. Its soft, plush quality makes it perfect for creating exquisite rugs with rich, detailed patterns.

Why It’s Unique:

The Tibetan knot allows for finer detailing and more complex designs due to its unique knotting technique. It’s often used for creating detailed, symbolic motifs and vibrant color patterns. The knots are tied with handspun wool, adding to the natural beauty of the rug.

Characteristics:

  • Luxurious texture with a soft and plush feel.
  • Intricate designs with vibrant colors and detailed motifs.
  • Ideal for artistic and symbolic patterns.

Durability:

Tibetan knot rugs are crafted with high-quality wool, making them durable and long-lasting. Despite their soft texture, they are capable of withstanding moderate foot traffic. The rich wool fibers provide warmth and resilience, making these rugs an investment for years to come.
