Types of Flat Weave

Kilim

Kilim is a traditional flat weave rug from the Middle East, known for its geometric patterns and vibrant colors.

Dhurrie

Dhurrie is a flat woven rug from India, famous for its durability and minimalistic design.

Soumak

Soumak weaving, from the Caucasus region, is known for its intricate, raised patterns and rich textures.

Berber

Berber rugs are woven by indigenous people in North Africa, known for their minimalist, geometric designs.
