Apple Rugs Service Policy: A Complete Ordering & Fulfillment Guide

At Apple Rugs, we are committed to delivering high-quality handmade rugs from our Nepal facility with transparency and customer satisfaction. Below is our step-by-step process for ordering, producing, and delivering your rugs.

1. Inquiry and Sample Request

  • What You Do: Contact us to discuss designs, materials (wool, silk), colors, and weaving techniques. Request a sample if needed.
  • What We Do: Provide detailed info and ship samples with tracking from Nepal.
  • Freight: Shipping cost depends on weight/size; prepaid or collect options available.

2. Sample Approval

  • What You Do: Review and approve or request changes.
  • What We Do: Implement feedback and confirm production specs.

3. Purchase Order (PO) Submission

  • What You Do: Submit PO with rug details: size, material, quantity, etc.
  • What We Do: Review and confirm alignment with production capabilities.

4. Proforma Invoice (PI) and Payment

  • What We Do: Issue PI with all order and payment details.
  • What You Do: Review and make advance payment via wire transfer.

5. Production Process

  • What We Do: Start production after payment. Includes:
    • Design finalization
    • Yarn dyeing
    • Hand weaving
    • Trimming, washing, and quality inspection
  • Production Updates: Weekly reports and optional photos.

6. Pre-Shipment Confirmation

  • What We Do: Confirm completion and send final photos.
  • What You Do: Approve or request minor changes (if feasible).

7. Shipping

  • What We Do: Ship rugs from Nepal and provide tracking info.
  • Delivery Timeline: Around 7 days to reach destination airport.

8. Shipment Insurance

  • What We Do: Inspect rugs before shipment; recommend insurance for transit safety.
  • What You Do: Arrange insurance through your provider.

9. Customs Clearance and Delivery

  • What You Do: Clear customs, pay duties/taxes, and arrange delivery.
  • What We Do: Provide required documents like invoice and packing list.

10. Post-Delivery Support

  • What You Do: Inspect rugs and share feedback.
  • What We Do: Address concerns promptly and ensure satisfaction.

Key Notes

  • Payment: Advance payment required via wire transfer (details provided in PI).
  • Privacy and Security: Your personal info is strictly confidential.
  • Satisfaction Guarantee: Each rug is inspected before shipment to meet your specs.
  • Contact Us: Reach out anytime with questions or assistance needs.
