Hotel Yak & Yati

Applerugs is proud to have completed a prestigious project for the renowned Yak & Yeti Hotel in Kathmandu. For this project, we supplied and delivered a wide range of high-quality tufted carpets tailored to meet the hotel’s elegant interior design standards. With a total of 144 pieces covering over 1400 square meters, this collaboration highlights our ability to execute large-scale custom flooring solutions with precision and excellence. The successful completion of this project reflects our commitment to craftsmanship, timely delivery, and exceptional client service.
